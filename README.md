## What is Pillow Bud?

Pillow Bud is a **completely free, open-source emotional wellness app** that listens to what's on your mind without judgment. It combines AI-powered conversations, meditation, breathing exercises, journaling, and wellness games to support your mental health journey.

Think of it as a friend who's always available to talk, meditate with you, or help you find calm when everything feels overwhelming.

## 💬 How It Works

### The AI Listens

When you talk to Bud, it:
1. Understands what emotion you're expressing
2. Adapts its response based on your mood
3. Remembers context from your conversation
4. Responds with genuine empathy (not generic templates)

**Example:**
- You: "I feel overwhelmed by work"
- Bud detects: Overwhelmed (intensity: 7/10)
- Bud responds: In "Gentle" mode with validation + helpful perspective

### Breathing Guides You

Real-time animations show you exactly how to breathe:

```
4-7-8 Relaxation Breathing:
├─ Inhale for 4 seconds (circle grows)
├─ Hold for 7 seconds (glow pulses)
├─ Exhale for 8 seconds (circle shrinks)
└─ Repeat for calm
```

### Meditation Soothes

Choose duration (3-30 min) + sound preference:
- Silent (just you and your thoughts)
- Nature sounds (ocean, rain, forest)
- Guided session (voice instruction)
- Binaural beats (optional science)

### Journal Reveals Patterns

Write freely. Pillow Bud:
- Tags emotions automatically
- Creates mood charts
- Shows what times you're most stressed
- Celebrates your journaling streaks

### Games Distract Productively

Play Pac-Man when you need a break:
- Collect pellets, avoid ghosts
- Build muscle memory and focus
- See your high score improve
- Actually reduces stress (not procrastination)

## ❓ FAQ

**Q: Is this really free forever?**
A: Yes. No hidden costs, no premium tier, no data selling. Open source means sustainable and transparent.

**Q: Is my data safe?**
A: Yes. All data encrypted on your device. No servers involved. You can delete everything anytime.

**Q: Can Pillow Bud replace therapy?**
A: No. It's a support tool, not a replacement. If you need professional help, please seek it.

**Q: Works offline?**
A: Yes, completely. After download, everything works without internet.

**Q: How many quotes?**
A: 10,000+ organized by category and emotion.

**Q: Can I customize?**
A: Yes! 25 themes, custom colors, font sizes, personality modes, and more.

**Q: What's with Pac-Man?**
A: Quick stress-relief games help. Studies show 5-10 minutes of gameplay reduces anxiety. Plus it's fun!
